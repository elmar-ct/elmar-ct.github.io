Lift your Views Up to grasp the facts

Personal, Familial, National, International and Universal. Such is the trajectory common to all of us. The journey which leads us from the local to the global, from the particular to the general, from the one-sided to the many-sided. In mathematics, this is the trend. Differential geometry is focused in investigating geometric properties that could be preserved by the transition from local to global. For Manifolds, which are the principal objects of geometric investigations, are defined on local charts whose collection forms an atlas. The concern therefore lies naturally in how to translate local properties such as convexity and curvature to the global ones; how to abstract the local concerns to global concerns. In Physiology,too, scholars came to understand the necessity to integrate the understanding of life-process from the subcellular level to the organic, systemic and communitarian levels; from the science of molecule to that of public health.

Thus, almost everyone are aware of this progressive trend which will enable us to see the system in its entirety. Frederick Engels said:

People will no longer be, as they are today, subordinated to a single branch of production, bound to it, exploited by it; they will no longer develop one of their faculties at the expense of all others; they will no longer know only one branch, or one branch of a single branch, of production as a whole[...]Industry controlled by society as a whole, and operated according to a plan, presupposes well-rounded human beings, their faculties developed in balanced fashion, able to see the system of production in its entirety. (The Principles of Communism)

However, after lifting up your views to understand the particular through the universal, it is necessary to go back to the local in order to do the practice. Such outlook is the dialectical conception of the world. The only way to grasp the universal resides in the understanding of the local; in turn the understanding of the global determines the understanding of the local. This down-up and up-down motion constitutes the driving force of historical progress of thought. In other words, use the facts to understand the generality of things; and use the generality of things to sharpen our understanding of the facts. This is communist world outlook.

Laws and Matters

Liberals view stipulate that some hidden laws are governing the universe;that once these laws are known, man would be able to predict and control, at least in theory, every single phenomenon in the world. In the same way as the ruling class of the feudal system saw divine hand in all instances of human life, the elites of the bourgeoisie believe in some form of ultimate Governing-Principle or the so called “Fundamental Laws of the universe” that would have been established once and for all eternity.

Let us then investigate some aspects of this.

Although matter evolution can be partially described by some definite laws that are known to us today, these laws are not absolute nor immutable, but maintained, shaped and changed by material conditions. Any qualitative change in matter results necessarily in a change in these laws. Liberals chief mistake lies precisely in this fact that they subject the matter to the diktat of the laws thereby ignoring that laws are born from matters, not matters from laws. In turn, these laws must return to matter in order to improve and develop the material conditions.

Indeed, the Christ would say, “the Sabbath was made for man, not man for the Sabbath.” (Mark 2:27). Better, he would add he came not to abolish but to fulfill the laws. But, this means that the Law alone is not enough in practical situations:it needs some kind of human intervention for it to bear the fruit of salvation.

There is a dialectics between the laws and the matters the laws are supposed to rule over.

President Xi Jinping referred to this very dialectics when he talked about the “Invisible hand” and the “Visible hand” in the sphere of the economy, that is “The Law of the Market” and “the Party’s rule”.

“Letting the market play the decisive role in allocating resources and letting the government better perform its functions are not contradictory.”(Governance of China, The “Invisible Hand” and the “Visible Hand”, May 26, 2014.)

Letting the Invisible hand of the Marked play its basic then decisive role at micro-economic level while strengthening the “scientific macro-economic control” by the Government and the Party, such is the conclusion reached by Chinese socialist market economy. Such is the core reform that lifted millions of Chinese people out of poverty. Materials conditions change the laws which in turn are used, through practice to develop and to improve the materials conditions.

The thinking that laws are the ultimate ruling entity of the universe, that if these laws were known, everything would have been predicted with 100% accuracy, such thinking is simplistic, partial, and mutilated view of the universe. Such view is mechanistic materialism. Marxian world outlook is not mechanistic but dialectic and historical materialism. We do not forget that, in a national society, Constitution is made by man not man by Constitution.

Are we then opposed to the laws? Don’t we strive to find the laws governing things?

No! We are not opposed to the laws. We are striving the discover the laws of things! But, this is only a half-way in our dialectical journey; we do not stop there. We have to bring these laws, use them in practice to enrich our experiences of the phenomena. We draw on lessons and correct our mistaken ideas about the laws we have found, and eventually correct these laws to better predict reality.
